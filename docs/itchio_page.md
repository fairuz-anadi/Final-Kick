## Title
**FINAL KICK**

## Short description / tagline
> One kick. One chance. Rewind, aim again, and wake the machine up for good.

## Description

You're the last worker in a factory that forgot how to move.

All you have is a busted device strapped to your leg. It does one thing: it
lets you kick something *really* hard — and for a few seconds after, scrub
back through time like dragging a video timeline, watching a ghost of what
almost happened.

Every room is a broken system waiting for one push. Line up your kick, watch
the dominoes fall, the gears catch, the wires spark, the vials blow — and if
the chain dies halfway, rewind, adjust the angle, and try again. The level
ends on the one kick that finally works: the **final kick**.

## How to play
- **HOLD SPACE** — charge your kick
- **RELEASE SPACE** — kick
- **MOUSE** — aim
- **HOLD R + LEFT / RIGHT** — scrub back and forth through time

## Features
- **Quantum Rewind** — an undo you can *scrub*, not just press
- **Momentum Transfer Matrix** — gears spin machinery, vials widen the blast, wire grids surge power along the chain
- **Spectacle Cam** — when the chain completes, the camera goes cinematic for the payoff
